What is Mehndley

A studio for wedding medleys — no studio required.

Building the perfect entrance mashup usually means an editor, a producer, and a lot of back-and-forth. Mehndley puts the whole workflow in your browser: find the songs, cut them to the beat, and stitch them into one continuous track that plays exactly how you imagined.

FAQ

Questions, answered.

Is Mehndley free to use?

Yes. Search, trimming, mixing and export are all free. Just open the app and start building your medley.

What kinds of songs can I add?

Anything you can find in the search — Bollywood, Punjabi, Desi pop and more. Use the quick tags or type any song or artist.

What's the difference between crossfade and relay mode?

Crossfade smoothly blends one song into the next. Relay mode plays a short signature hook (like a dhol pattern) between every pair of songs, with optional silence gaps for dramatic pauses.

Can I save my work and come back later?

Yes — save any medley as a project, then reload, preview or re-export it whenever you like.

What format is the final file?

A single high-quality 320 kbps MP3 that plays on any phone, laptop, DJ setup or speaker.

Does it work on my phone?

Mehndley runs in any modern browser. For heavy trimming and editing, a laptop or desktop gives you the most room to work.

Can I use the medleys I make?

Mehndley is a personal tool for creating medleys for your own private events — your mehndi, baraat or reception. The songs remain the property of their respective artists and rights holders. Please don't use exported medleys for commercial purposes or public redistribution, and support the original artists.
